Important Information:

For Educational and Research Purpose Only. Not For Clinical Use.

Step 1: Load Plan File.

  • Drag and drop your DICOM RT Plan (.dcm) or exported JSON (.json) file onto the designated area below, or use the "Choose File" button.

Step 2: Configure Machine Limits (Optional). Adjust the machine speed limits to see how they affect the calculated delivery time. Click "Apply & Recalculate" to update.

Step 3: Interact with the Simulation.

  • Use the **Play/Pause Simulation** button to control the animation for the beam selected in the dropdown menu.
  • Check the **Simulate Full Plan** box to animate the entire plan from start to finish. The beam selector will be disabled in this mode.
  • Enable **Beep per MU** for audible feedback during playback.
  • Hover your mouse over the **Radial Plots** or move the **Control Point Selector** slider to scrub through the plan and view data for a specific control point.

CP Density

Control Point Density (CPs/°). The number of control points within a 1-degree gantry angle window, normalized to CPs per degree.

Dose Rate

MIsport

Modulation Index (sport model). Measures modulation incorporating leaf travel, gantry rotation, and dose rate variation between neighboring control points.

Local MIt

Local Modulation Index (Temporal). A score indicating MLC positional complexity and machine constraints within a localized time window of the simulation.

SAS (Small Aperture)

Small Aperture Score. Measures the prevalence of small aperture shapes, weighted by MU. Higher scores indicate more frequent use of small apertures relative to the plan's maximum.

AAV (Area Variability)

Aperture Area Variability. A measure of how much the shape of the aperture changes from one control point to the next. Lower values indicate more variability and complexity.

MCSv (Modulation)

Modulation Complexity Score (coll-adj). A combination of Aperture Area Variability (AAV) and Leaf Sequence Variability (LSV). Lower values indicate higher overall modulation complexity.
